Well, who am I?

Well, that’s a great question. I don’t really know myself.

If you know french, here is a reflexion essay I made in highschool for my philosophy course: https://files.thetrevor.tech/trevor/misc/qui-suis-je-philos.pdf

I wanted to start off by explaining the whoami Linux (gnu coreutils) command first. But I’m sure it’s self explanatory. Computer systems have users. The command just prints who is the user running the command.

But well… who am I ? That’s one of philosophy’s most-searched questions.

See the history of this blog post: https://git.thetrevor.tech/blog.git/log/articles/me-why.md
